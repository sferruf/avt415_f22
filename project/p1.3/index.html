Irene Goodman has recently taken an interest to learn how to knit. She wants to knit herself a sweater but she doesn't know how to. Irene browses the VELVET website in search of the right yarn to use, the correct needles, buttons, markers, and an easy pattern to follow. Irene finds a beginner-friendly knitting kit that will work perfectly with the purple mohair yarn skeins she selected and placed in her cart. She is ready to make her purchase and places her order.
